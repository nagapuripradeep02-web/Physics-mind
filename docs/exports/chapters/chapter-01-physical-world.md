## Teaching pathway (dependency-ordered)

### Level 0 — foundations

- **`fundamental_forces_in_nature_atomic`** — The **four fundamental forces**: gravitational, electromagnetic, strong nuclear, weak nuclear. Relative strengths (~1 : 10⁻² : 10⁻¹³ : 10⁻³⁸, taking strong = 1) and ranges (gravity + EM infinite; strong ~10⁻¹⁵ m; weak ~10⁻¹⁸ m). EVERY force in nature is one of these four.  _(targets misconception: dozens of different forces)_
  - ↳ `four_forces_strength_range_table_nano` — The comparison table: strong (1, ~10⁻¹⁵ m), EM (10⁻², infinite), weak (10⁻¹³, ~10⁻¹⁸ m), gravity (10⁻³⁸, infinite). **Simulatable as a strength-bar + range-scale visual.** Why gravity dominates cosmically (infinite range + always attractive) despite being weakest.
  - ↳ `all_contact_forces_are_em_nano` — Friction, tension, normal reaction, spring force, drag — ALL are the electromagnetic repulsion/attraction between the electron clouds of surface atoms. **The single most unifying aha**: the macroscopic contact-force zoo of T11/T12 is ONE force (EM) in disguise. Genuine "small → smaller" recursion.
  - ↳ `unification_story_nano` — The drive toward unification: Maxwell unified electricity + magnetism + optics into electromagnetism; the electroweak theory unified EM + weak; the search for grand unification + quantum gravity continues. Physics seeks the fewest principles explaining the most phenomena.
- **`conservation_laws_as_organizing_principles_atomic`** — Conservation of **energy, linear momentum, angular momentum, and charge** as the DEEP organizing principles of physics — rooted in symmetries of nature (qualitative Noether: time-symmetry → energy; space-symmetry → momentum; rotational-symmetry → angular momentum). More fundamental than any specific force law: they hold even when the detailed forces are unknown.  _(targets misconception: conservation law = just one more formula)_
  - ↳ `conservation_from_symmetry_nano` — Qualitative Noether: every conservation law mirrors a symmetry of nature. Energy ← laws don't change over time; momentum ← laws don't change across space; angular momentum ← laws don't change with orientation. **Why conservation is DEEP, not arbitrary.**
  - ↳ `conservation_makes_problems_solvable_nano` — Conservation laws let you relate "before" and "after" WITHOUT tracking the messy forces in between (e.g. collision: momentum before = momentum after, no need to integrate the contact force). This is WHY they are the physicist's first tool.
- **`scope_and_domains_of_physics_atomic`** — The reach of physics across **scale** (10⁻¹⁵ m nucleus → 10⁺²⁶ m observable universe, ~41 orders of magnitude) and the **three domains of validity**: CLASSICAL (everyday sizes/speeds), QUANTUM (atomic/sub-atomic), RELATIVISTIC (speeds near c). Each theory is the correct model WITHIN its domain.  _(targets misconception: classical mechanics is wrong, replaced by quantum/relativity)_
  - ↳ `scales_of_the_universe_nano` — The scale ladder: quark < proton (10⁻¹⁵ m) < atom (10⁻¹⁰) < human (10⁰) < Earth (10⁷) < solar system (10¹³) < galaxy (10²¹) < observable universe (10²⁶). Links to T2 order-of-magnitude estimation. **Simulatable as a logarithmic zoom.**
  - ↳ `domains_of_validity_nano` — Classical mechanics is EXACT for everyday scales/speeds (a cricket ball, a car); quantum is needed at atomic scale; relativity at speeds near c. The boundaries (not right-vs-wrong) — classical is a limiting case of both.
  - ↳ `physics_technology_society_nano` — Physics → technology → society loop: ISRO (orbital mechanics + EM), DAE nuclear power (nuclear forces), semiconductor/IT (quantum). The Indian national-science narrative. **Marketing-relevant framing**: physics is the engine behind India's strategic capabilities.
