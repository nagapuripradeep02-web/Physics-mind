## Slide 2 — The Problem

**Understanding science shouldn't depend on whether you can afford a great teacher.**

- A great personal tutor is the biggest advantage in education — and the rarest
- Passive video and text don't make abstract concepts *click*
- Students memorize formulas without understanding — misconceptions go uncorrected
- 1-to-1 human tutoring is expensive and doesn't scale

> Say: "The real gap isn't content — it's access to a teacher who makes it click and corrects how you think."

- An **AI professor** that explains, answers doubts by voice, and controls the simulation live
- *"ChatGPT can tell you; PhysicsMind shows you"* — ask "what if the charge were negative?" and the simulation itself answers

## Slide 4 — Why It's Innovative  *(the EXIST-critical slide)*

**Not another tutoring app — a deep-tech engine.**

- Pre-built **physics engines** do all computation; the AI writes only *configuration*, never the physics → always accurate, no hallucinated science
- A **curriculum-specific library of concept simulations** — hard to build, and our core IP
- A **proprietary data flywheel**: every student doubt makes the next explanation better — an asset no competitor can copy

> Say: "The innovation is the architecture and the data — that's what makes it defensible, not a feature anyone can clone."

---

## Slide 5 — Product & Business Model  *(B2C)*

**Direct-to-student subscription.**

- **B2C:** students subscribe directly (web app + AI voice professor)
- **Tiered:** concept simulations → + AI doubt-solving → + voice AI professor
- **Concept clarity first** (serves every student); deeper exam prep grows with the student over time

> Say: "We sell directly to students. We're not dependent on schools or institutions."
