## Section G — Cognitive-Error-Prevention Decisions

**3 of 8 founder decisions are cognitive-error-prevention type = 38%.** Below the Kinematics-cluster high band (48.5%) but within the foundation-topic range — even the qualitative intro chapter carries genuine convention/mental-model misconceptions.

| Tag | cognitive_error_target | Countermeasure |
|---|---|---|
| **PW-G3** | "there are dozens of different forces (friction, tension, normal, spring, drag...)" | Mandatory framing: only TWO forces act at everyday scale (EM for ALL contact forces + gravity); strong/weak act only in the nucleus. The force-zoo collapses to 4 fundamentals |
