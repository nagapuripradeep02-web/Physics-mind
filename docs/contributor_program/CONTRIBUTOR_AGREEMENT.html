1. The work

The Contributor will review PhysicsMind physics simulations and deliver “Packets”. One Packet for one simulation consists of: (a) a recorded First-Look session (thinking aloud while watching the simulation), (b) a recorded Teaching session (teaching the concept using the simulation), and (c) a completed State-by-State Review Sheet (verdict, reason, suggested fix, and recording timestamp for every state).

The Contributor Guide (shared separately) describes the quality requirements. The Contributor works as an independent contractor — this is not employment, and it is unrelated to any other platform or tutoring service the Contributor may use.

2. Recording consent

The Contributor consents to the recording of review and teaching sessions (screen + voice). Recordings of sessions with the Contributor’s own students require the Contributor to obtain the student’s (or parent’s) consent; such recordings capture the Contributor’s voice and the simulation screen only.

3. Ownership and use of deliverables

All deliverables — recordings, transcripts, review sheets, comments, and suggestions — become the sole property of the Company upon payment, as work made for hire. The Company may use them for any purpose, including improving the simulations and developing/training AI teaching systems. The Contributor’s personal identity will not be published without separate written permission.

4. Payment

₹500 (five hundred rupees) per accepted Packet, paid by UPI within 48 hours of acceptance. A Packet is accepted when it meets the Contributor Guide checklist (complete states, specific reasons, timestamps, usable recordings). One round of fixes may be requested; the Company will state exactly what is missing. No payment is due for Packets that remain incomplete after the fix round, or where the simulation was opened before the First-Look session.

5. Confidentiality

Simulation links, content, internal documents, and unreleased product details are confidential. The Contributor will not share access links, keep recording copies for personal use, or describe unreleased features publicly while this agreement is active and for 12 months after.

6. General

Either side may end this arrangement at any time with a simple message; accepted Packets up to that date will be paid. This agreement is governed by the laws of India. Replying “I AGREE — [full name, date]” to this document on WhatsApp or email counts as signature.
