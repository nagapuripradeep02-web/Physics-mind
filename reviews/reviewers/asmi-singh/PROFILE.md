## Working style (learned so far)

- The originally-designed live review protocol was too hard to follow. She proposed and we adopted an
  **async flow**: she reviews the sim solo + writes notes in an **Excel sheet**, then we do a
  recorded **teach-back call** where she walks through her feedback as if teaching a Class-12 student.
- Found the **TTS narration too fast**.
- Keeps feedback **deliberately limited per pass** — won't dump every possible change at once ("if I
  suggest 5 things in one go it gets too complicated"). Reviews iteratively: fix → re-review →
  smaller second pass.
- Strong on pedagogy/sequencing — catches *order-of-introduction* problems, not just surface bugs.
