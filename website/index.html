About

Made with teachers, in classrooms.

Every simulation is shaped by professors teaching real students, reviewed state by state, before it ships. If it doesn’t survive a live classroom, it doesn’t go in.

The physics is accurate by construction: rendering engines compute each frame from the governing equations, in real 3D. Nothing is key-framed, nothing is approximated art — so what your students see is what the mathematics says.

And it’s built for Indian classrooms first — NCERT-mapped, exam-aware, comfortable in bilingual teaching. You stay the teacher; is the picture.
